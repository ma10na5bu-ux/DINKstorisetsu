```
Draw a wide illustration (16:9 aspect ratio, 1200×630px).

【Scene】
A Shiba Inu dog (red/tan color) sitting regally on the exact center of a sofa cushion with a stern, authoritative expression — like a tiny boss enforcing rules. Around the dog, visual cues of "my rules": the dog's specific spot on the sofa is slightly worn/indented (it's always THIS spot), a pair of socks on the floor that the dog has claimed, a dog toy placed precisely in a specific corner, and a food bowl that has been nudged to a very particular angle. A human stands nearby with hands on hips, looking at the dog with a "you're the real boss, aren't you?" expression. The dog looks back with zero compromise.

【Art Style】
- Flat vector illustration style, NOT anime
- Minimal line work, soft rounded shapes
- Similar to modern web/app illustration (like Notion or Slack illustrations)

【Color Palette — STRICT】
- Background: soft warm gray (#F5F0EB)
- Primary: muted sage green (#8BA888)
- Secondary: warm beige (#D4C5B2)
- Accent (minimal use): soft coral (#D4907E)
- Skin tones: warm natural tones
- NO bright red, NO bright blue, NO vivid yellow, NO saturated colors

【Composition】
- Clean and simple, lots of white space
- The authoritative Shiba Inu on the sofa is the focal point, centered
- The scattered "rule evidence" items around the room tell the story
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT place any important elements in the bottom-right corner (the bottom-right 15% of the image will be cropped)
- Magazine editorial feel, calm and sophisticated

【Rules】
- No text, no watermarks, no logos
- Keep all important elements within the center 60% of the image
- Overall tone: calm, warm, minimal
```
