```
Draw a wide illustration (16:9 aspect ratio, 1200×630px).

【Scene】
A split composition. On one side, a computer screen displays a generic, perfect-looking digital dog image (flat, lifeless, symmetrical). On the other side, a real Shiba Inu dog (red/tan color) is doing something wonderfully imperfect — mid-yawn with one eye squinted, tongue out, ears asymmetrical, fur slightly ruffled. The real dog radiates warmth and personality. A person is reaching past the computer screen to pet the real dog, clearly choosing the messy, imperfect, living creature over the perfect digital version. Small heart icons float around the real dog. The computer screen looks cold and flat in comparison. The mood says "nothing beats the real thing."

【Art Style】
- Flat vector illustration style, NOT anime
- Minimal line work, soft rounded shapes
- Similar to modern web/app illustration (like Notion or Slack illustrations)

【Color Palette — STRICT】
- Background: soft warm gray (#F5F0EB)
- Primary: muted sage green (#8BA888)
- Secondary: warm beige (#D4C5B2)
- Accent (minimal use): soft coral (#D4907E)
- Skin tones: warm natural tones
- NO bright red, NO bright blue, NO vivid yellow, NO saturated colors

【Composition】
- Clean and simple, lots of white space
- The contrast between the lifeless digital dog and the vibrant real Shiba is the focal point, centered
- The person reaching for the real dog drives home the article's message
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT place any important elements in the bottom-right corner (the bottom-right 15% of the image will be cropped)
- Magazine editorial feel, calm and sophisticated

【Rules】
- No text, no watermarks, no logos
- Keep all important elements within the center 60% of the image
- Overall tone: calm, warm, minimal
```
