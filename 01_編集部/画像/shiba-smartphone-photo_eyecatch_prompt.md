# アイキャッチ画像プロンプト
## うちの柴犬の写真が全部ブレてる問題を解決した話

**保存ファイル名**: `shiba-smartphone-photo_eyecatch.png`
**トリミング**: `trim-eyecatch.py` で処理

---

```
Draw a wide illustration (16:9 aspect ratio, 1200×630px).

【Scene】
A person lying flat on the ground (shown from the side), holding a smartphone at ground level, trying to take a photo of a Shiba Inu dog (red/tan color). The Shiba is sitting a short distance away with a perfect "shiba smile" — mouth open, eyes squinted happily. On the phone screen (visible to the viewer), the photo shows a perfectly framed, non-blurry shot of the dog. Scattered around the person are visual hints of failed attempts: a few small "blurry photo" icons floating nearby (simple rectangles with motion blur lines, no text). The contrast between the effort (lying on the ground) and the result (perfect shot) is the humor. The mood is lighthearted and relatable.

【Art Style】
- Flat vector illustration style, NOT anime
- Minimal line work, soft rounded shapes
- Similar to modern web/app illustration (like Notion or Slack illustrations)

【Color Palette — STRICT】
- Background: soft warm gray (#F5F0EB)
- Primary: muted sage green (#8BA888)
- Secondary: warm beige (#D4C5B2)
- Accent (minimal use): soft coral (#D4907E)
- Skin tones: warm natural tones
- NO bright red, NO bright blue, NO vivid yellow, NO saturated colors

【Composition】
- Clean and simple, lots of white space
- The person on the ground with phone and the smiling Shiba create a humorous scene, centered
- The blurry photo icons add the "before vs after" story
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT place any important elements in the bottom-right corner (the bottom-right 15% of the image will be cropped)
- Magazine editorial feel, calm and sophisticated

【Rules】
- No text, no watermarks, no logos
- Keep all important elements within the center 60% of the image
- Overall tone: calm, warm, minimal
```
