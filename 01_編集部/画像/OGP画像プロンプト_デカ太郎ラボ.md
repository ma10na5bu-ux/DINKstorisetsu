## 案A：リビングの3人（サイトの世界観を一枚で伝える・王道）

```
Draw a wide illustration (16:9 aspect ratio, 1200×630px).

【Scene】
A cozy living room scene. A young Japanese couple in their 30s sitting together on a sofa, relaxed and happy. The husband (short black hair, average build) is leaning forward with a curious, excited expression, pointing at something on a laptop. The wife (brown bob hair) sits beside him with a calm, gentle smile. A small Shiba Inu dog (red/tan color) is curled up between them on the sofa, looking content and unbothered. The room has warm afternoon light from a window, a coffee table with two mugs, and a small potted plant.

【Art Style】
- Flat vector illustration style, NOT anime
- Minimal line work, soft rounded shapes
- Similar to modern web/app illustration (like Notion or Slack illustrations)

【Color Palette — STRICT】
- Background: soft warm gray (#F5F0EB)
- Primary: muted sage green (#8BA888)
- Secondary: warm beige (#D4C5B2)
- Accent (minimal use): soft coral (#D4907E)
- Skin tones: warm natural tones
- NO bright red, NO bright blue, NO vivid yellow, NO saturated colors

【Composition】
- Clean and simple, lots of white space
- The couple and dog are centered in the image
- Magazine editorial feel, calm and sophisticated
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT place any important elements in the bottom-right corner (the bottom-right 15% of the image will be cropped)

【Rules】
- No text, no watermarks, no logos
- Keep all important elements within the center 60% of the image
- Overall tone: calm, warm, minimal
```

## 案C：俯瞰リビング（おしゃれ・ブランド感重視）

```
Draw a wide illustration (16:9 aspect ratio, 1200×630px).

【Scene】
A top-down (bird's eye view) illustration of a cozy living room floor. In the center, a Japanese couple in their 30s are sitting on a rug. The husband (short black hair) is lying on his back reading a magazine, looking relaxed. The wife (brown bob hair) sits cross-legged with a coffee mug. A small Shiba Inu dog (red/tan color) is stretched out sleeping between them. Around them: an open laptop, a small camping lantern, a houseplant, scattered lifestyle magazines, and a dog toy.

【Art Style】
- Flat vector illustration style, NOT anime
- Minimal line work, soft rounded shapes
- Similar to modern web/app illustration (like Notion or Slack illustrations)
- Isometric or flat top-down perspective

【Color Palette — STRICT】
- Background: soft warm gray (#F5F0EB)
- Primary: muted sage green (#8BA888)
- Secondary: warm beige (#D4C5B2)
- Accent (minimal use): soft coral (#D4907E)
- Skin tones: warm natural tones
- NO bright red, NO bright blue, NO vivid yellow, NO saturated colors

【Composition】
- Clean and simple, balanced layout
- The couple and dog form the visual center
- Surrounding items tell the story of the blog's topics (outdoor, lifestyle, money, dog)
- Magazine editorial feel, calm and sophisticated
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT place any important elements in the bottom-right corner (the bottom-right 15% of the image will be cropped)

【Rules】
- No text, no watermarks, no logos
- Keep all important elements within the center 60% of the image
- Overall tone: calm, warm, minimal
```

## 選び方

| 案 | 印象 | 向いているケース |
|---|---|---|
| A（リビングの3人） | 安心感・暮らし感・王道 | 「夫婦＋豆柴の暮らしブログ」が一目で伝わる |
| B（お出かけ前） | ワクワク・動き・日常感 | 「調べて試して」の行動的な面を出したい |
| C（俯瞰リビング） | おしゃれ・ブランド感 | デザイン性重視。ブログのテーマ（犬・キャンプ・暮らし）を小物で網羅 |
